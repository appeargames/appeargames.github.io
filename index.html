War System

Blue Army

Command the Blue Army to capture strategic resource points and expand your territory. Each base displays "power points" representing your military strength.

Factory + Base Link

When a base is adjacent to a factory, it automatically generates troops! A steady stream of reinforcements for your frontlines.

Resource Transfer

Transport helicopters quickly move "power points" between friendly bases, enabling strategic resource allocation to turn the tide of battle.
